Overview

REECHA Inc. has been in operation since 2016 and has become a reputable service provider with a proven track record in delivery of quality service to our ever-growing clientele. REECHA is a modern small to medium sized professional service firm established by young black professionals with vast experience in public practice.

Our skills base comprises of CA (SA)s, CIAs, CFEs and CISAs. The growth of our organisation has also created opportunities in human resources, marketing, and information technology and office administration. With increased growth, we aim to recruit and grow our staff complement even further in the next three years.

We are busy establishing a footprint throughout the country with offices in the Eastern Cape – East London and Northern Cape Province – Kimberly. We offer tailor-made services to our clients in:
• Assurance engagements (Audit and non-audit)
• Governance Risk and Compliance
• Taxation
• Forensics and Special Investigations
• Consulting and Advisory
• Technology Risk Services
