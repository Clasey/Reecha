Who We Are

REECHA Inc. has been operating since 2016, providing high-quality professional services by young black professionals.

Our staff includes CAs, CIAs, CFEs, and CISAs, ensuring comprehensive service delivery and industry expertise.

We have offices in Eastern Cape and Northern Cape, offering services in Assurance, GRC, Taxation, Forensics, Consulting, and Technology Risk Services.
